This is my rendition of a Fibonacci Clock. Its a cool way to tell time, based on the Fibonacci sequence. small squares are worth 1 each. Then, the values of the squares increase based on size (2,3,5). To tell time you add up the squares. If a square is blue, it counts towards the hours. If it is green, it counts towards minutes. A red square counts for both hours and minutes. To figure out the time, sum the hours and minutes. Minutes need to be multiplied by 5 after they're summed (so it is only accurate within 5 minutes). The pictured screenshot represents 4:30. You can find that project here!
